## [Subversive Software](https://subversivesoftware.org/)

Building hopeful, human-focused software that gives control back to people. We
believe in privacy as a right, transparency by default, and technology that serves
people. We build tools that give people visibility and help them understand how
technology is being used around them better.

Examples include:
- Tapped - a network visibility tool
- Survey - a tool that makes it easy to see bluetooth, wifi and software defined radio
- F0 - a Mac app for Flipper Zero
- Flack - A browser extension that lays a trail of waste behind
- Proxy
- Webring - An easy way to share information across sites.
